# D365 Client-Side UI Kit

A portable client-side kit for Dynamics 365 and Dataverse: native-looking
(refreshed UCI, Fluent UI v9) custom UI with code-level control, shipped across
webresources, PCFs, and form scripts from one shared library (React 18 plus
TypeScript).

This Storybook is the kit's visual contract. Each control runs here so its look,
states, and behavior are reviewable side by side with the native model-driven
controls they stand in for.

## The three tiers

**Smart controls.** The metadata-aware tier, and the one to look at first. A smart
control resolves its label, options, format, currency, and lookup behavior from
Dataverse metadata through a host context, then threads plain values into a
presentational control underneath. In Storybook they run against an in-memory
metadata fake (no org), so their resolution is visible without a live environment.

**Presentational controls.** The CRM-agnostic building blocks the smart tier
renders underneath: values in, events out, no host and no metadata. They render
the native UCI look over fixture data, which is why every one of them runs in
Storybook with zero mocks.

**Sample patterns.** Composed scenarios that show the tiers working together for
the cases a single native control cannot cover: a merged grid over heterogeneous
sources, a unified activities list, a master and detail layout, a multi-step
wizard, and more.

## How to read a story

Open a control, then the **Show code** panel under each sample is the real usage:
the props a host passes and the shape of the data it supplies. Presentational
stories take fixtures directly; smart stories take an entity and attribute and let
the metadata fake do the resolving.
